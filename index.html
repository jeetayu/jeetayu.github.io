Research Focus

Our work investigates the molecular mechanisms of RNA-binding protein (RBP) function, alternative pre-mRNA splicing, and mRNA regulation in cancer. We develop single-molecule and genomic tools — including HyperTRIBE and MS2-TRIBE — to map RBP–RNA interactions at transcriptome scale in living cells. A major focus is understanding how splicing factor mutations (e.g., SF3B1) drive aberrant splicing programs in myeloid malignancies (MDS/AML).
