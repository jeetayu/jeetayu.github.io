Synthetic Duplexes (for guide RNA annealing)

  1. Mix 2 µL each oligo (100 µM) + 4 µL 2X TES → final 25 µM each
  2. Heat 95°C 1 min on PCR machine, then ramp down slowly to 4°C
  3. Dilute 10x into 100 mM NaCl; use 1.25 µL per ligation reaction

Agarose Gel Electrophoresis

  • % = g/100 mL (1% = 1 g per 100 mL TAE or TBE); each gel ≈ 50 mL
  • Add 5 µL ethidium bromide per 50 mL after cooling to ~60°C
  • Run: 100V in 1× TAE/TBE; check at 20–30 min
  • 1.5% gel for colony PCR (fast); 1% for larger fragments

Restriction Digest

  • Use 3 µg DNA per digest for sufficient material downstream
  • CutSmart buffer (NEB) is compatible with most enzymes — check NEB website for exceptions
  • Incubate 37°C (or enzyme-specific temperature); maximum 1 hour
  • Heat inactivate 20 min at 65°C
  • Add 2 µL 0.5 M EDTA pH 8.0 before gel extraction to completely inactivate

CIP Dephosphorylation (blunt-end backbones)

  • 1 µL CIP in CutSmart buffer; total 100 µL
  • Maximum 1 hour at 37°C then heat inactivate

Heat Shock Transformation

  1. Thaw competent cells on ice; add 1–5 µL ligation or plasmid DNA
  2. Ice: 20 min
  3. 42°C water bath: exactly 45 seconds
  4. Ice: 2 min to recover
  5. Add 500 µL SOC; shake 37°C at 300 rpm for 45–60 min
  6. Plate 100 µL (and remainder if needed) onto selective plates
  7. Incubate overnight at 37°C
DH5α: 50 µL cells (can split each tube into 2 plates). Rosetta: 25 µL cells (pRare requires Chl + Amp plates).

Reconstitute Plasmid from Filter Paper

  1. Cut paper with fresh razor blade
  2. Place in Eppendorf with 50 µL sterile dH₂O
  3. Soak 5 min at RT
  4. Use 10 µL for transformation; store remainder at -20°C

Reverse Transcription

SuperScript IV (for 15 reactions)

Step 1 — Primer annealing MM:

  • 15 µL 50 µM oligo-dT or random hexamer primer
  • 15 µL 10 mM dNTP mix
  • Add 2 µL of this MM to each tube containing 11 µL RNA
  • Anneal: 65°C 5 min → ice 1 min

Step 2 — RT MM:

  • 60 µL 4X SSIV Buffer + 15 µL 100 mM DTT + 15 µL RNaseOUT + 15 µL SSIV enzyme
  • Add 7 µL per tube
  • 55°C 10 min → 80°C 10 min (inactivation)
  • Dilute 1:5 by adding 80 µL nuclease-free water; store at -20°C

LunaScript RT MM (simplified)

  • 4 µL master mix + 16 µL RNA sample
  • 25°C 2 min → 55°C 10 min → 95°C 1 min

Protein Purification — Rosetta Cell Expression

Transformation & Induction

  1. Transform 50 µL Rosetta cells; plate on Amp + Chl plates (pRare has Chl resistance)
  2. Pick single colony → 50 mL LB starter culture overnight
  3. Inoculate 10 mL into 1L LB in Fernbach flask
  4. Grow at 37°C; check OD₆₀₀ after 2–3 h. When OD >0.2, thaw IPTG
  5. At OD 0.6: add IPTG to 1 mM; induce 4h at 37°C (or 16°C overnight)
  6. Spin 15 min at 6,000 × g; store pellet at -80°C

Bacterial Lysis (xTractor Buffer)

  1. Add 20 mL xTractor buffer per 1 g pellet; resuspend
  2. Add 40 µL DNase I (1 U/µL) + 200 µL 100× lysozyme (optional)
  3. Gentle mixing; incubate 10 min at RT with shaking
  4. Centrifuge 10,000–12,000 × g, 20 min, 4°C; save supernatant

TALON Spin Column Purification (His-tag)

All centrifuge steps: 700 × g, 2 min.

  1. Remove storage buffer from column (snap seal; centrifuge into 2 mL collection tube)
  2. Equilibrate: add 1 mL equilibration buffer 3×, centrifuge each time
  3. Load 0.6–1 mL clarified lysate; agitate gently 5 min
  4. Centrifuge; save flowthrough (check on gel)
  5. Wash 3×: add 1 mL equilibration buffer; agitate 5 min; centrifuge
  6. Elute 2–3×: add 400–600 µL elution buffer; agitate; centrifuge; collect in fresh tube

Run gel: Crude lysate — Flowthrough — Wash 1–3 — Elutions 1–3

Amylose Column Purification (MBP-tag, Large-Scale Gravity)

Buffers

BufferKey Components (per 100 mL)
Lysis Buffer25 mM Tris pH 7.2 + 1.5 M NaCl + 1 mM EDTA + 1 mM DTT (if Cys) + 1 protease inhibitor tablet + 1 µL lysozyme at time of lysis
Wash Buffer (1L)25 mM Tris pH 7.2 + 300 mM NaCl + 1 mM EDTA + 1 mM DTT (if Cys)
Elution Buffer (200 mL)25 mM Tris pH 7.2 + 300 mM NaCl + 1 mM EDTA + 1 mM DTT + 10 mM Maltose (0.72 g)

Protocol

  1. Resuspend pellet in 40 mL cold lysis buffer; rock in cold room 20 min
  2. Equilibrate amylose column (pump 150–200 mL wash buffer at 2 mL/min)
  3. Sonicate: 30 s ON / 2:30 OFF × 10 min total; power 3.5; on ice
  4. Centrifuge 15,000 × g, 20 min, 4°C; load supernatant onto column at 0.5 mL/min
  5. Wash 4 hours at 2 mL/min
  6. Elute: 10 × 12 mL + 1 × 40 mL final elution at 2 mL/min

TEV Cleavage (to remove MBP tag)

  • Add MBP-TEV to elutions in TEV dialysis buffer; incubate overnight
  • Confirm cleavage by gel (PRE vs POST-TEV)
  • Run on amylose gravity column to capture free MBP-TEV; cleaved protein flows through
  • Dialyze flowthrough into crystallization/storage buffer
TALON His-tag column run (for dual MBP/His constructs) follows same wash protocol but uses PBS + imidazole buffers. Do not let column run dry during final elution. Store in 20% ethanol.
