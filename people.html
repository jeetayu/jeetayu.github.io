Principal Investigator
JB
Jeetayu Biswas, M.D., Ph.D.
Principal Investigator • MSKCC

Research focused on post-transcriptional gene regulation and RNA biology in cancer. Work spans RNA-binding protein target identification (HyperTRIBE/MS2-TRIBE), alternative pre-mRNA splicing in hematologic malignancies, mRNA localization, and single-molecule imaging. Previously in the Singer lab (Einstein) and Rosbash lab (Brandeis); now at MSKCC in the Abdel-Wahab lab studying splicing in MDS/AML.
